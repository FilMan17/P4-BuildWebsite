Full Description

The Yamaha MT-09 stands as a refined evolution of Yamaha's iconic hyper-naked lineup. Known for its versatility, thrilling performance, and rider-centric features, the MT-09 is a balanced choice for enthusiasts seeking both daily usability and spirited rides. Its updated design, advanced technology, and refined ergonomics position it as a class leader in the naked bike segment, appealing to a wide range of motorcyclists.


Engine and Performance: The 2024 MT-09 is powered by Yamaha’s renowned 890cc CP3 inline-triple engine, delivering a dynamic blend of power and responsiveness. The engine produces 115 horsepower and 68 lb-ft of torque, providing exhilarating acceleration and a rich midrange. Enhanced by refined intake ducts and a revised airbox, the engine produces a more immersive sound experience. The updated quickshifter now supports seamless upshifts and downshifts, further enhancing performance and ride smoothness at all speeds.


Chassis and Handling: The chassis incorporates a twin-spar aluminum frame with the engine as a stressed member for optimal weight distribution and rigidity. Updates to the suspension include a firmer KYB 41mm inverted fork and a revised rear linkage for smoother shock travel, offering improved cornering stability and a comfortable ride. The lower, wider fuel tank design and repositioned handlebars bring more weight to the front tire, enhancing low-speed maneuverability and high-speed control, making it equally adept at city navigation and aggressive riding.


Braking and Safety: The braking system features dual 298mm front discs with radial-mount four-piston calipers and a 245mm rear disc, upgraded with a premium Brembo master cylinder for improved lever feedback and precision. Advanced rider aids, including cornering ABS and lean-sensitive traction control, ensure safety without compromising performance. Slide control and wheelie control add further confidence, especially during spirited rides.


Design and Features: The 2024 MT-09 boasts a bold new aesthetic, highlighted by its redesigned Predator-esque front end with dual-function LED headlights and position lights. The reshaped fuel tank and two-piece seat enhance its aggressive profile, while thoughtful details like larger mirrors and forged components improve functionality. The modernized bodywork ensures it stands out as a statement of style and engineering.


Ergonomics and Comfort: Rider comfort is significantly improved with a flatter and narrower seat, adjustable handlebars, and repositioned footpegs. These adjustments cater to a wide range of rider sizes while providing a more integrated feel with the bike. The ergonomic changes create a balanced riding position that reduces fatigue during longer rides.


Technology and Features: The MT-09 integrates Yamaha’s most advanced technology to date, featuring a 5-inch TFT display with four modes and smartphone connectivity via the Yamaha Y-Connect app. The app provides turn-by-turn navigation and allows customization of riding aids. With three preset modes (Sport, Street, Rain) and two customizable options, riders can fine-tune parameters like throttle response and traction control to match their preferences. Standard cruise control adds convenience for highway riding.


Conclusion: The Yamaha MT-09 continues to set benchmarks in the naked bike category by blending performance, technology, and rider comfort into a cohesive package. Its versatility and character-rich design make it a compelling choice for riders seeking an engaging motorcycle for urban commutes, weekend thrills, and longer adventures alike.
