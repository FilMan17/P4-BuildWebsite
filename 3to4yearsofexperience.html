Category Description

The "3-4 years of experience" motorcycle rider category includes individuals who have developed solid riding skills through consistent practice and exposure to diverse riding conditions over several years. These riders are comfortable handling a range of motorcycles, have refined techniques like cornering, braking, and throttle control, and are experienced in navigating both urban and highway environments. They may be exploring higher-performance bikes and advanced skills such as group riding, long-distance touring, or track days to further enhance their expertise.

Sport Bike

Bike Description

The Kawasaki ZX-6R is a high-performance supersport motorcycle powered by a 636cc inline-four engine, delivering thrilling acceleration and sharp responsiveness for both street and track enthusiasts. With advanced features like updated electronics, rider modes, and traction control, it offers precise control and adaptability to various riding conditions. Its aerodynamic design, aggressive styling, and lightweight chassis make the ZX-6R a standout choice for riders seeking a premium combination of speed, agility, and modern technology.
