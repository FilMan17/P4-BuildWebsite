Full Description

The 2024 Honda Africa Twin continues its legacy as one of the most versatile and capable adventure motorcycles in the market. Combining rugged off-road capability with highway touring comfort, it caters to riders seeking adventure on any terrain. Available in both the standard Africa Twin and the more touring-oriented Adventure Sports ES variant, the bike boasts updates that enhance its performance and usability, making it a standout choice for long-distance travel enthusiasts.


Engine and Performance: Powered by a refined 1,084cc liquid-cooled parallel-twin engine with a Unicam design, the Africa Twin delivers smooth yet powerful performance. The engine features a revised intake and exhaust system, as well as a 10.5:1 compression ratio, ensuring responsive power delivery. It offers 4 riding modes—Tour, Urban, Gravel, and a user-customizable option—alongside Honda’s Selectable Torque Control for optimal adaptability across varying terrains. The optional Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT) enhances its versatility, offering seamless gear changes for both road and off-road adventures.


Chassis and Handling: The Africa Twin’s chassis emphasizes stability and maneuverability. Its lightweight frame is paired with a Showa suspension system, featuring an inverted fork and Pro-Link monoshock that provide ample travel for off-road adventures. The Adventure Sports ES variant further improves handling with the electronically adjustable Showa suspension, allowing riders to fine-tune damping and preload on the fly. The standard model retains its 21-inch front and 18-inch rear wheels, ensuring excellent off-road traction, while the Adventure Sports ES features a new 19-inch front wheel for improved on-road handling.


Braking and Safety: Braking is handled by dual 310mm front discs with 4-piston calipers and a 256mm rear disc with a 2-piston caliper. Both models include cornering ABS with switchable on-road and off-road modes, providing confidence in diverse riding conditions. The braking system is designed to deliver reliable stopping power without compromising control, even during aggressive off-road riding.


Design and Features: The 2024 Africa Twin showcases a rugged yet aerodynamic design. Its updated fairing and five-position windscreen improve wind protection, while tubeless tires enhance convenience during long rides. The Adventure Sports ES features a larger 6.6-gallon fuel tank for extended range, heated grips for added comfort, and a taller windscreen, making it ideal for touring.


Ergonomics and Comfort: Ergonomically, the Africa Twin ensures comfort for extended journeys. With a standard seat height of 34.3 inches (adjustable to a lower position of 33.5 inches), it accommodates a wide range of riders. The Adventure Sports ES offers additional adjustability, with a lower seat height and enhanced touring ergonomics. Both models prioritize rider and passenger comfort with well-padded seats and optimized handlebar positioning.


Technology and Features: The 6.5-inch touchscreen display is a highlight, offering Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and Bluetooth connectivity. Riders can easily access navigation, media, and bike settings through an intuitive interface. Cruise control comes standard, reducing fatigue during long rides, while the Adventure Sports ES benefits from advanced suspension settings accessible through the display.


Conclusion: The Honda Africa Twin and its Adventure Sports ES variant are a testament to Honda’s commitment to adventure motorcycling. With its powerful yet manageable engine, advanced electronics, and thoughtful ergonomics, it is an ideal choice for riders seeking a bike that seamlessly transitions between challenging off-road trails and comfortable highway cruising. Whether you’re an adventure enthusiast or a long-distance tourer, the Africa Twin offers an unmatched blend of capability, comfort, and reliability.
