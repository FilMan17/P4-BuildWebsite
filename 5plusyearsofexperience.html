Category Description

The "5+ years of experience" motorcycle rider category represents veteran riders who have honed their skills through extensive practice and exposure to a wide range of riding environments and bike types. These individuals are highly proficient in advanced techniques such as high-speed cornering, emergency braking, and managing challenging road conditions, and they are comfortable riding both solo and in groups. With a deep understanding of motorcycle mechanics, performance nuances, and safety protocols, veteran riders often seek out high-performance bikes, long-distance touring, or track riding, continually pushing their limits and knowledge of the sport.

Sport Bike

Bike Description

The BMW S1000RR is a high-performance supersport motorcycle powered by a 999cc inline-four engine, delivering exceptional power and precision for track enthusiasts and serious riders. With cutting-edge technology including dynamic traction control, race-spec ABS, and a quick-shifter, it offers unmatched agility, handling, and adaptability across various riding conditions. Its aggressive design, lightweight frame, and advanced aerodynamics make the S1000RR a top choice for those seeking a thrilling, top-tier motorcycle with unparalleled speed and control.

Specifications:

  • Displacement: 999cc

  • Weight: 434lbs (197kg)

  • Horsepower: 205hp

  • Torque: 83ft-lbs (113Nm)

  • Top Speed: 195mph (314kmh)

  • 0-60mph: 3.1 seconds

Naked Bike

Bike Description

The Yamaha MT-10 is a raw and aggressive naked bike powered by a 998cc inline-four engine, delivering exhilarating power and torque for riders who crave performance. Equipped with advanced electronics like traction control, ride modes, and quick-shifter, it provides a dynamic riding experience tailored to different conditions and rider preferences. Its muscular design, lightweight frame, and exceptional handling make the MT-10 a standout choice for riders seeking both thrilling performance and everyday practicality.

Specifications:

  • Displacement: 998cc

  • Weight: 467lbs (212kg)

  • Horsepower: 164hp

  • Torque: 82.6ft-lbs (112Nm)

  • Top Speed: 161mph (259kmh)

  • 0-60mph: 3.0 seconds

Cruiser

Bike Description

The 2024 Harley-Davidson Road King is a classic touring cruiser, powered by a Milwaukee-Eight 107 V-twin engine that offers a smooth and powerful ride, perfect for long-distance cruising. With its iconic styling, comfortable ergonomics, and features like a large front fork, advanced infotainment system, and optional cruise control, it combines performance with premium touring amenities. The Road King’s relaxed riding position, plush seat, and sleek design make it an ideal choice for riders seeking both comfort and style on extended rides.

Specifications:

  • Displacement: 1868cc

  • Weight: 828bs (376kg)

  • Horsepower: 95hp

  • Torque: 122ft-lbs (165Nm)

  • Top Speed: 110mph (177kmh)

  • 0-60mph: 5.5 seconds
