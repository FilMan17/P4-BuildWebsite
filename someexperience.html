Category Description

The "some experience" category of motorcycle riders includes individuals who have prior exposure to riding but are not yet fully proficient with motorcycles. These riders may have experience handling dirt bikes, mopeds, or scooters, giving them a basic understanding of throttle control, balance, and steering. While they are familiar with the fundamentals of two-wheel vehicles, they may still be developing confidence with larger bikes, manual transmissions, or more complex riding environments like busy streets or highways.

Sport Bike

Bike Description

The Kawasaki Ninja 500, is a versatile and beginner-friendly sportbike powered by a 451cc parallel-twin engine that delivers smooth, approachable performance. Its lightweight frame, moderate seat height, and responsive handling make it an excellent choice for new riders transitioning to larger motorcycles or those seeking a practical commuter bike. Combining sporty aesthetics with reliability and efficiency, the Ninja 500 offers a balance of fun and functionality for riders of varying skill levels.

Specifications:

  • Displacement: 451cc

  • Weight: 379lbs (172kg)

  • Horsepower: 51hp

  • Torque: 31.7ft-lbs (43Nm)

  • Top Speed: 120mph (193kmh)

  • 0-60mph: 4.8 seconds

Naked Bike

Bike Description

The Yamaha MT-03 is a lightweight, naked sportbike powered by a 321cc parallel-twin engine that delivers smooth, responsive performance ideal for urban commuting and spirited riding. With its aggressive styling, upright riding position, and agile handling, it offers a blend of comfort and excitement for newer riders or those seeking a fun, manageable motorcycle. Equipped with modern features like LED lighting and an LCD display, the MT-03 combines practicality with a bold, dynamic presence on the road.

Specifications:

  • Displacement: 321cc

  • Weight: 373lbs (169kg)

  • Horsepower: 37.1hp

  • Torque: 21.8ft-lbs (29.5Nm)

  • Top Speed: 108mph (174kmh)

  • 0-60mph: 5.11 seconds

Cruiser

Bike Description

The Kawasaki Vulcan S is a stylish, mid-sized cruiser powered by a 649cc parallel-twin engine, delivering smooth and responsive performance with plenty of torque for city and highway riding. Its unique Ergo-Fit system allows riders to customize the seat, handlebars, and footpegs for optimal comfort and fit, catering to a wide range of riders. With its modern design, easy handling, and relaxed riding position, the Vulcan S offers a versatile and approachable cruiser experience.

Specifications:

  • Displacement: 649cc

  • Weight: 498lbs (226kg)

  • Horsepower: 61hp

  • Torque: 46.3ft-lbs (62.8Nm)

  • Top Speed: 124mph (200kmh)

  • 0-60mph: 4.2 seconds
