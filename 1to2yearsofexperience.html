Category Description

The "1-2 years of experience" motorcycle rider category includes individuals who have developed foundational riding skills and gained confidence through regular practice over a year or two. These riders are comfortable with the basics of motorcycle operation, including clutch control, gear shifting, and cornering, and have likely experienced diverse riding conditions, such as urban traffic, highways, and varying weather. While still honing advanced techniques like high-speed cornering or emergency maneuvers, they are ready to explore more powerful or versatile bikes that challenge their growing skills.

Sport Bike

Bike Description

The Yamaha YZF-R7 is a sleek, mid-level sportbike powered by a 689cc CP2 parallel-twin engine, delivering a perfect balance of torque, smoothness, and responsiveness. With its lightweight chassis, aggressive R-Series styling, and a comfortable yet sporty riding position, it caters to both track enthusiasts and street riders. Equipped with features like adjustable suspension and advanced braking systems, the R7 offers precision handling and a thrilling experience for riders seeking a step up in performance.

Specifications:

  • Displacement: 689cc

  • Weight: 414lbs (188kg)

  • Horsepower: 73hp

  • Torque: 49.4ft-lbs (67Nm)

  • Top Speed: 134mph (216kmh)

  • 0-60mph: 3.81 seconds

Naked Bike

Bike Description

The Yamaha MT-07 is a versatile naked motorcycle powered by a 689cc CP2 parallel-twin engine, renowned for its punchy torque and smooth, linear power delivery. With a lightweight chassis, upright riding position, and agile handling, it excels in both city commuting and spirited backroad riding. Combining bold, aggressive styling with practicality and affordability, the MT-07 is a favorite among beginner and experienced riders alike.

Specifications:

  • Displacement: 689cc

  • Weight: 406lbs (184kg)

  • Horsepower: 73hp

  • Torque: 49.4ft-lbs (67Nm)

  • Top Speed: 137mph (220kmh)

  • 0-60mph: 3.71 seconds

Cruiser

Bike Description

The Kawasaki Vulcan 900 is a classic mid-sized cruiser powered by a 903cc V-twin engine, offering smooth and reliable performance with plenty of low-end torque. Its timeless cruiser styling, comfortable low seat, and spacious ergonomics make it ideal for relaxed highway cruising or city rides. With features like a belt drive, generous fuel capacity, and high-quality finishes, the Vulcan 900 delivers a premium riding experience at an approachable price point.

Specifications:

  • Displacement: 903cc

  • Weight: 620lbs (281kg)

  • Horsepower: 51.2hp

  • Torque: 60.2ft-lbs (81.6Nm)

  • Top Speed: 115mph (185kmh)

  • 0-60mph: 5.92 seconds
