Full Description

The 2024 Harley-Davidson Road King is a masterclass in blending timeless cruiser aesthetics with modern touring capabilities. Renowned for its iconic design, this motorcycle caters to riders seeking a balance between the elegance of traditional Harley styling and the practical features required for long-distance travel. With its imposing presence, the Road King serves as a quintessential touring bike, ideal for riders who demand comfort and performance on the open road.


Engine and Performance: At the heart of the Road King lies the Milwaukee-Eight 114 V-Twin engine, delivering 122 ft-lbs of torque at 2,750 rpm and 95 horsepower at 5,020 rpm. This powerhouse provides smooth acceleration and effortless cruising at highway speeds. Paired with a six-speed transmission, the engine offers excellent fuel economy at approximately 43 mpg, making it a reliable partner for extended journeys.


Chassis and Handling: The Road King features a robust chassis designed for stability and smooth handling, even with a fully loaded touring setup. A low-slung design combined with a 26-degree rake ensures balance and control, while its premium suspension system—comprising a 49mm dual-bending valve front fork and hand-adjustable rear shocks—absorbs road imperfections with ease. The result is a plush and confidence-inspiring ride.


Braking and Safety: Safety is paramount with advanced braking technologies, including a standard Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and Electronic Linked Braking (ELB). Optional cornering-enhanced systems provide superior control during turns, while features like Vehicle Hold Control (VHC) and optional Traction Control Systems add further assurance on varied terrains.


Design and Features: The Road King retains its classic Harley-Davidson silhouette, complemented by modern touches such as LED lighting and blacked-out finishes. Its stretched saddlebags offer 2.5 cubic feet of storage, making it perfect for touring enthusiasts. A signature batwing fairing and minimalist aesthetic maintain the bike's vintage appeal while integrating contemporary functionality.


Ergonomics and Comfort: Designed with long-distance comfort in mind, the Road King offers a plush, well-contoured seat and wide handlebars for an upright, relaxed riding posture. The low 695mm seat height ensures accessibility for a range of riders, and the spacious footboards enhance overall comfort during extended rides.


Technology and Features: The motorcycle is equipped with modern conveniences, including cruise control and an optional tire pressure monitoring system (TPMS). Its digital-analog gauges display vital information such as trip data, range, and gear indicator. Harley’s optional Rider Safety Enhancements suite introduces cutting-edge features like Cornering Drag-Torque Slip Control for improved handling.


Conclusion: The Harley-Davidson Road King perfectly encapsulates the spirit of open-road freedom. With its combination of timeless design, powerful performance, advanced safety features, and unparalleled comfort, it continues to set the standard for touring motorcycles. Whether navigating city streets or embarking on cross-country adventures, the Road King delivers an experience that's quintessentially Harley-Davidson.
